Weeks 1-4: Building Foundation

Day Focus Exercises
Day 1 Shoulders & Back Overhead Press, Lateral Raises, Pull-ups, Cable Rows, Dumbbell Shrugs
Day 2 Chest & Arms Bench Press, Incline Press, Bicep Curls, Tricep Pushdowns, Chest Fly
Day 3 Legs & Core Squats, Leg Press, Lunges, Standing Cable Crunches, Hanging Leg Raises
Day 4 Shoulders & Back Arnold Press, Wide Pull-ups, Seated Row, Kettlebell Swings
Day 5 Chest, Arms & Core Incline Press, Cable Chest Fly, Standing Barbell Curls, Dips
Day 6 Cardio & Lower Body Jogging, Leg Press, Deadlifts, Standing Calf Raises
Day 7 Rest / Recovery Active recovery, yoga, or light cardio

Weeks 5-8: Progression & Intensification

Day Focus Exercises
Day 1 Shoulders & Back Progressively add weight, focus on form
Day 2 Chest & Arms Add supersets, increase intensity
Day 3 Legs & Core Increase reps or weight, add explosive movements
Day 4 Shoulders & Back Focus on width, progressive overload
Day 5 Chest, Arms & Core Keep intensity high, supersets with compound exercises
Day 6 Cardio & Lower Body Focus on fat burning and leg development
Day 7 Rest / Recovery Stretch or perform light activity
